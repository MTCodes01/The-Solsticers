What Our Project Is

Students, Designers & Thinkers

Our project brings the excitement of space exploration to the web with a 3D interactive view of exoplanets, the Habitable Worlds Observatory (HWO). Users can dive into two exploration modes: a telescope simulation that mimics what HWO would see, or a free-roam mode with game-like controls to explore the galaxy however they like. In telescope mode, you can tweak settings to see how exoplanets might look, making it both fun and educational. It's a chance to explore the possibilities of finding habitable worlds in a simple but engaging way!
